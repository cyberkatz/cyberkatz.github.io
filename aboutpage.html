About

Our mission is to grow and foster a community for students passionate about all things revolving around computer security, or lack thereof :). All UMD students, regardless of major, are welcome to join and participate in CSEC. No prior experience with cybersecurity is required. Request to join our TerpLink organization, join the club Discord (see previous newsletters), attend our club meetings, and compete in Capture the Flag (CTF) competitions. In order to view the link to our Discord, one must sign-in to Terplink and request to join the club!
